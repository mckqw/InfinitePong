Chinese

Beginning in High School Matthew began to study the Chinese language. After a few shortcomings in the classroom he found his stride in individual study and practice with his fellow Chinese friends. After two years of initial studying he commited to his first trip to China. He went to Xi'an on an exchange program with his local High School, Saugus High School. After relocationg to Mclean, Virginia with his family, He continued his study in and outside of the classroom. This lead to his second trip to China. Which included an initial tour of ShangHai and BeiJing with his family. Afterwhich he began to work as an English tutor untill he returned. His most recent trip also included visits to ShangHai and BeiJing, however; this trip served a different pourpose. He was there for the gospel's sake. This has had a life changing impact on his outlook on the future and what Chinese means to Matthew today.
